FAQs

How does it work?

We want to curate a community for high-slope operators. That means not everyone will be admitted. It’s as simple as applying to join by clicking the “create a profile” button. From there, you’ll be able to see who else is interested in building and you can connect with them directly.

Tell me more about the events.

We’ll regularly host curated and intimate events for the community. We believe that every great company was built with the help of others. So, join our events to jam on ideas, meet potential teammates, and connect with people who’ve been there and done that.

Do I have to pay?

No, cofounders.nyc is free!

Why NYC?

We love NYC and even though the NYC tech ecosystem has had some big wins over the years like Datadog, MongoDB, AppNexus, and so many others, it’s still finding its groove. Tons people are moving here from SF every day and we felt that it needed a dedicated digital and in-person community to help spur some action.
